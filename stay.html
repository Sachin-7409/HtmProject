Get PUMPED for the wild run- Forsyth Trail Run.

For the First Time in Central India, Moustache is organizing a 40 Km Jungle Marathon collaborated with MP Tourism. With a max of 30 runners only, we invite you to race the century-old trail, following all the COVID guidelines. Come, join us to reconnect with nature, crossing the deepest corners of the Satpura rainforest and the thrills of gaining an elevation of 1000m (approx.) running by rocky terrains, riverbeds, and hidden natural pools & falls on the way. So why back off, when you got the chance to challenge yourself for a healthier future in 2022. A journey full of great adventures, inviting all the Pro-Athletes to enroll for this non-profit attempt. An initiative for a better & greener lifestyle.
